OUR RECIPE

Marinate 4 chicken pieces in buttermilk for at least 2 hours, then coat them in a seasoned flour mix of paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and cayenne. Heat oil to 350°F, fry the chicken for 10-12 minutes per side until golden and crispy, ensuring the internal temperature reaches 165°F. Let it drain on paper towels before serving hot. Enjoy the ultimate crunch!
